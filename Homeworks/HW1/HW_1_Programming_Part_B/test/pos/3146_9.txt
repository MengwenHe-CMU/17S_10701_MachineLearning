In the world of  shorts   most of which aren't   this film is a gem  A quiet  concise peek into the world of a young woman who's a reader for a blind woman  here the stellar Elizabeth Franz - this film bears the textures  layers and visual storytelling of a sumptuously painted still life  The dialogue is minimal  the cinematography is stunning  and the direction sure  clear and compelling  I saw this film in a film festival held in a loud and crowded Tribeca bar - and within the first two minutes  and for the first time that night   the crowd fell quiet  That says it all
